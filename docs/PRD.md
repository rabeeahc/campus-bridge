# Campus Bridge - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Overview

Campus Bridge is a web platform designed to help international students navigate university life before arriving, during their first weeks, and throughout their studies.

Instead of searching through WhatsApp groups, university websites, Reddit posts, and asking random people, students can find everything they need in one place.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

International students often struggle to find reliable, organized, and up-to-date information about university life and living in a new country.

Common problems include:

- Applying for a residence permit (ikamet)
- Opening a bank account
- Buying a SIM card
- Understanding transportation
- Registering for courses
- Finding good professors
- Accessing previous exam papers
- Finding affordable housing and furniture
- Discovering good restaurants and cafés
- Finding libraries, hospitals, pharmacies, and places of worship
- Connecting with other international students
- Knowing who to ask for help

Most of this information exists, but it is scattered across different websites, social media groups, and word of mouth.

Campus Bridge brings it together into one platform.

---

## 3. Target Users

Primary Users:
- Newly admitted international students
- Current international students

Secondary Users:
- Senior students
- Student clubs
- International Office
- University ambassadors

---

## 4. Goal

Help international students feel prepared, informed, and connected from the moment they receive their university acceptance letter.

---

## 5. Success Metrics

The project will be successful if students can:

- Find answers faster
- Spend less time searching for information
- Connect with other students easily
- Discover useful places around the city
- Feel more confident during their transition

---

## 6. Out of Scope (Version 1)

The first version will NOT include:

- Messaging
- AI Assistant
- Mobile application
- Video calls
- Payments

These can be added in future versions.

---

## Core Principles

1. Solve real student problems.
2. Keep information simple and easy to find.
3. Make students feel less alone.
4. Prioritize verified and trustworthy information.
5. Build with scalability in mind.
